=== Entrepreneurship Iteration Log ===
Timestamp: 2026-04-26_12-41-45
Focus Area: Low-barrier digital services requiring minimal upfront investment
Trend Summary: 
- Many small businesses still rely on manual methods (phone, paper) for appointments and customer management.
- Growing demand for simple digital solutions that integrate with popular messaging platforms like LINE (in Taiwan) or WhatsApp elsewhere.
- Low coding barrier due to no-code tools and platform APIs.
Generated Ideas:
1. LINE 預約系統：讓小型服務業（理髮店、美容院）透過 LINE Official Account 接受預約，自動提醒。
2. 數位名片服務：提供簡單網頁製作工具，讓個人快速建立可分享的數位名片，支援社群連結。
3. 社群訂閱新聞紙：針對特定興趣（如都市園藝、手作）製作精選內容，使用 Substack 或 Beehiiv 平台。
4. 社區活動報名平台：讓區里長或社區幹部發布工作坊課程，參加者可線上報名並行程同步。
5. QR code 忠誠卡：商家生成專屬 QR code，顧客掃描累積點數，可兌換折扣。
Selected Idea: LINE 預約系統給小型服務業
Validation:
- Target customer: Independent hair salons in Taipei with 1-3 stylists
- Problem: Missed calls, double-bookings, no-shows
- Solution: LINE Official Account where customers can book via rich menu, get automatic reminders
- Channels: Visit salons in person, offer free setup for first 3 months
- Revenue model: NT$500/month per salon after free trial
- Biggest assumption: Salons will pay for a system that reduces no-shows
Next steps: Mock up the LINE rich menu flow and visit 5 salons to gauge interest.
