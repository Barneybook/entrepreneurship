Selected Idea: AI-powered appointment reminder system for local service providers
Validation:
- Target Customer: Independent hair salons, barbershops, beauty clinics with 1-5 practitioners in urban areas
- Problem Solved: High no-show rates (typically 20-30%), missed phone calls during business hours, double-booking errors
- Solution: Simple SMS-based system where customers can book, reschedule, or cancel appointments via text. AI understands natural language requests and sends automatic reminders 24 hours and 2 hours before appointment.
- Channels: Direct salon visits with free trial offer, partnerships with beauty supply stores, Google My Business optimization
- Revenue Model: Subscription model: NT$300/month per salon after 2-week free trial, volume discounts for chains
- Biggest Assumption: Salons will pay for a system that reduces no-shows, and that SMS-based interaction is sufficient for their booking needs

Next Steps: Create a simple prototype using Twilio API for SMS and test with 3-5 local salons to validate the no-show reduction assumption.
