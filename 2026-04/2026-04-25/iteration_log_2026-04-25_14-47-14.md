Selected Idea: No-code appointment booking system for freelance professionals
Validation:
- Target customer: Freelance professionals (consultants, tutors, therapists, coaches) who bill by the hour and need clients to book sessions.
- Problem solved: Missed booking opportunities, back-and-forth emails to schedule time, no-shows, and lack of a professional online presence.
- Solution: A simple, branded booking page where clients can see availability and book slots automatically synced to the freelancer's calendar. Includes automatic reminders via email or SMS.
- Channels: Direct outreach via LinkedIn, Facebook groups for freelancers, offering free setup for first 3 months in exchange for testimonials.
- Revenue model: Subscription: $5/month per freelancer after a 2-week free trial. Alternatively, transaction fee of 5% per booking.
- Biggest assumption: Freelancers are willing to pay a small monthly fee for a tool that saves them time and reduces no-shows.

Next steps: Create a landing page using Carrd.co with a mockup of the booking interface. Reach out to 10 freelancers in personal network to gauge interest and collect feedback on pricing.
