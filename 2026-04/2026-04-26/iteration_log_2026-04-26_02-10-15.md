Selected Idea: Simple LINE-based appointment system for hair salons
Validation:
- Target customer: Independent hair salons in Taipei with 1-3 stylists that use LINE for customer communication.
- Problem solved: Missed calls, double-bookings, no-shows; reliance on phone-based appointment system.
- Solution: LINE Official Account where customers can view available slots via rich menu, book instantly, get automatic reminders, and reschedule if needed.
- Channels: Visit salons in person, offer free setup for first 3 months; partner with beauty supply stores for referrals.
- Revenue model: NT$500/month per salon after free trial; potential add-ons for premium features (e.g., customer loyalty program, online payments).
- Biggest assumption: Salons will pay for a system that reduces no-shows and saves staff time, and that customers will adopt LINE booking over phone calls.

Next Steps: 
1. Mock up the LINE rich menu flow and booking process.
2. Visit 5-10 hair salons in Taipei to gauge interest and collect feedback on pain points.
3. Build a minimal viable product using LINE Messaging API and a simple booking calendar (could use Google Calendar API or a lightweight database).
4. Offer a free 3-month pilot to salons that show interest, in exchange for feedback and testimonials.
