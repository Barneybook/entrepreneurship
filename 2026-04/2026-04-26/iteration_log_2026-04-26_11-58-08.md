Selected Idea: Platform for industry-specific micro-certifications in emerging tech (e.g., AI prompt engineering, low-code development)
Validation:
- Target customer: Individuals seeking to upskill in emerging tech fields without committing to long courses; employers looking to verify specific skills.
- Problem solved: Gap between traditional education and rapidly evolving skill requirements; need for granular, verifiable skill proof.
- Solution: Online platform offering short, focused courses (2-4 hours) with practical assessments; upon passing, learners receive a blockchain-secured digital badge shareable on LinkedIn/resume.
- Channels: Partnerships with tech communities, LinkedIn targeting, content marketing via blog/tutorials demonstrating the skills.
- Revenue model: Course fees (one-time per certification), bulk licenses for companies, premium subscription for access to multiple certifications.
- Biggest assumption: Learners will pay for micro-certifications if they perceive clear career benefit and the credential is recognized by employers.
