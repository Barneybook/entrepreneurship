Selected Idea: Simple appointment/booking system via LINE or WhatsApp for service-based businesses
Validation:
- Target Customer: Independent service businesses in Taiwan (hair salons, beauty parlors, tutors, therapists) with 1-5 staff.
- Problem Solved: Missed calls, double-bookings, no-shows, time spent managing appointments manually.
- Solution: LINE Official Account where customers can book via rich menu, get automatic reminders, and reschedule.
- Channels: Visit businesses in person, offer free setup for first month; also advertise on local Facebook groups.
- Revenue Model: NT$300/month per business after free trial; or transaction fee per booking.
- Biggest Assumption: Businesses will pay for a system that reduces no-shows and saves time.
Next Steps: Mock up the LINE rich menu flow and visit 5 businesses to gauge interest.
