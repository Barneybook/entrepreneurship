Selected Idea: LINE-based appointment reminder service for small beauty salons and barbershops in Taiwan
Validation:
- Target customer: Independent beauty salons and barbershops in Taipei and other major cities in Taiwan, with 1-5 staff.
- Problem: They rely on phone books or memory for appointments, leading to missed calls, double bookings, and no-shows.
- Solution: A LINE Official Account that allows customers to book appointments via a rich menu, receives automatic reminders, and allows rescheduling.
- Channels: Direct sales visits to salons, offering a free trial for 3 months, and referral discounts.
- Revenue model: NT$500 per month per salon after the free trial.
- Biggest assumption: Salons will see value in reducing no-shows and are willing to pay a small monthly fee.
