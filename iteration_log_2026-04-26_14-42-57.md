Selected Idea: AI-powered appointment and reminder system for local service businesses
Validation:
- Target Customer: Independent service businesses (salons, clinics, repair shops) with 1-5 employees in urban areas.
- Problem Solved: Missed calls, double-bookings, no-shows, and time spent on manual appointment management.
- Solution: An AI chatbot integrated with LINE Official Account that allows customers to book, reschedule, or cancel appointments via natural language. The system sends automatic reminders and can handle common queries.
- Channels: Direct outreach to local business associations, offering free trial for 3 months, partnerships with local chambers of commerce.
- Revenue Model: Subscription fee: NT$500/month per business after free trial. Additional premium features (e.g., advanced analytics) at NT$300/month.
- Biggest Assumption: Business owners are willing to trust an AI system with their appointment booking and will see a reduction in no-shows and administrative time.

Next Steps: Create a prototype of the LINE rich menu and conversation flow. Visit 10 local service businesses to gauge interest and collect feedback on the prototype.
