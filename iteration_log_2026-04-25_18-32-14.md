Selected Idea: AI Menu Assistant for Restaurants
Validation:
- Target Customer: Independent restaurants and cafes with 1-10 staff, particularly those without online ordering systems
- Problem Solved: Staff spending excessive time answering repetitive menu questions (ingredients, allergies, recommendations), leading to slower service and potential errors
- Solution: A WhatsApp/LINE bot that customers can message with menu questions. The bot uses AI to understand questions about ingredients, allergies, spice levels, and can make recommendations based on preferences.
- Channels: Direct outreach to local restaurants, partnerships with restaurant supply stores, local business associations
- Revenue Model: Subscription model: NT$300/month per restaurant after free trial, with volume discounts for chains
- Biggest Assumption: Restaurants will see enough value in reducing staff time on menu questions to pay for the service

Next Steps:
- Create a simple prototype using WhatsApp Business API or LINE Official Account
- Visit 10 local restaurants to gauge interest and collect common menu questions
- Test the AI response accuracy with real menu data
- Offer free trial to 3 restaurants in exchange for feedback
