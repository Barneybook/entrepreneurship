Selected Idea: LINE/Whatsapp-based appointment booking for small service providers
Validation:
- Target customer: Independent hair salons, beauty studios, tutors, trainers.
- Problem solved: Missed calls, double bookings, no-shows, manual scheduling.
- Solution: Customers send a message to check availability, book, get reminders, and can reschedule.
- Channels: Direct outreach to local businesses, offer free trial, social media ads targeting business owners.
- Revenue model: Subscription fee per month (e.g., NT$300) after free trial, or transaction fee per booking.
- Biggest assumption: Businesses will pay for a tool that reduces no-shows and saves time.

Next steps: Create a simple prototype using no-code tools (e.g., Tally for booking, Zapier for reminders) and test with 5 local businesses.
