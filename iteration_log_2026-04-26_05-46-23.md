Selected Idea: AI-powered social media content generator for local Taiwanese restaurants
Validation:
- Target Customer: Small to medium Taiwanese restaurants (especially family-run) that have a LINE Official Account but post irregularly. They would find this via restaurant associations, Facebook groups, or LINE ads.
- Problem Solved: Inconsistent social media presence leads to lower customer engagement and missed promotion opportunities due to lack of time and skills for content creation.
- Solution: AI service that generates weekly social media post drafts (text and image suggestions) based on restaurant type, location, and upcoming events. Owner reviews, approves, and schedules with one click via LINE Official Account.
- Channels: Direct outreach to restaurant owners, partnerships with restaurant supply associations, Facebook groups for F&B businesses in Taiwan, targeted LINE official account ads.
- Revenue Model: Subscription NT$300/month per restaurant after a 7-day free trial. Potential tiered pricing for premium features (e.g., custom branding, analytics).
- Biggest Assumption: Restaurant owners will trust AI-generated content that matches their brand voice and are willing to pay for the convenience.
